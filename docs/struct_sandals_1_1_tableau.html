Detailed Description

template<Size S>
struct Sandals::Tableau< S >

Class container for the Butcher tableau of a Runge-Kutta methos.

Class container for the Butcher tableau of a Runge-Kutta methos. The Butcher tableau of a Runge-Kutta method of order \( p \) is defined as

\[ \begin{array}{c|c} \mathbf{c} & \mathbf{A} \\ \hline & \mathbf{b}^\top \\ & \hat{\mathbf{b}}^\top \end{array} \text{,} \]

where \( \mathbf{A} \) is the Runge-Kutta matrix

\[ \mathbf{A} = \begin{bmatrix} a_{11} & a_{12} & \dots & a_{1s} \\ a_{21} & a_{22} & \dots & a_{2s} \\ \vdots & \vdots & \ddots & \vdots \\ a_{s1} & a_{s2} & \dots & a_{ss} \end{bmatrix} \text{,} \]

\( \mathbf{b} \) is the Runge-Kutta weights vector

\[ \mathbf{b} = \left[ b_1, b_2, \dots, b_s \right]^\top \text{,} \]

\( \hat{\mathbf{b}} \) is the (optional) embedded Runge-Kutta weights vector

\[ \hat{\mathbf{b}} = \left[ \hat{b}_1, \hat{b}_2, \dots, \hat{b}_s \right]^\top \text{,} \]

and \( \mathbf{c} \) is the Runge-Kutta nodes vector

\[ \mathbf{c} = \left[ c_1, c_2, \dots, c_s \right] \text{.} \]
